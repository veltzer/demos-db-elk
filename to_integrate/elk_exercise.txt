ELK =
	Elastic Search *
	LogStash
	Kibana *

In this exercise we DO NOT USE:
	- the fulltext capabilities of Elastic
	- LogStash
We do use:
	- Elastic as database
	- Kibana as visualization on top of Elastic

- Install ELK on your virtual machine.
- Make sure you can see the web interface.
- Insert some data into your ELK (this means into your Elastic Search).
	Make sure the data has a time stamp and some number associated with it.
	16 Oct 2021 17:32 PM, 67
	17 Oct 2021 07:32 PM, 97
- Visualize this data using a Kibana Panel.
